About us

Dz Food is one of the first original Algerian restaurants in Netherlands. The restaurant is furnished with cozy low couches and large decorative ornate pillows. The rooms are softened by tapestries covering the walls and ceilings. This decor creates an atmosphere of comfort and leisure. The meal is a 5 course feast served with fresh home baked bread. This is more than just a meal; you can give over an entire evening to this relaxed and unique dining experience.
